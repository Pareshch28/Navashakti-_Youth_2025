Our Journey & Vision

Since its establishment in 2012, Navashakti Youth has been a vibrant community of the Nayi Brahman Samaj, dedicated to the worship of Goddess Durga Devi during Navaratri. For 11 days, we come together in devotion with yagnas, cultural programs, and energetic dandiya nights, celebrating both spirituality and tradition.

Our roots trace back to the proud legacy of the Sarvaajanik Hatti Ganesh Mandali, founded in 1921, which has stood as a symbol of unity and devotion for over 100 years. Inspired by their path, Navashakti Youth continues this tradition—uniting one and all through faith, culture, and community service.

Our Journey in Numbers

  • Years of Devotion: 13+ Years
  • Days of Navaratri Celebration: 11 Days Every Year
  • Cultural Legacy: 100+ Years (since 1921)
  • Community Growth: Stronger every year in faith & unity

🌞 Day 4: Kushmanda Puja – Celebrating the Goddess of Creation

👩‍👦 Day 5: Skandamata Puja – Paying homage to the Mother of Skanda

⚔️ Day 6: Katyayani Puja – Worshipping the Warrior Goddess

🌌 Day 7: Kalaratri Puja – Honoring the Goddess of Auspiciousness

✨ Day 8: Mahagauri Puja – Celebrating the Goddess of Purity

🙏 Day 9: Siddhidatri Puja – Seeking blessings from the Goddess of Accomplishments
